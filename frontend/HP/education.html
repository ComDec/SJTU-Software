An intelligent platform for DNA nano contriving

Education

We went to society and class,conducting science popularization of DNA and related computing application principles for students and any other people who’re interested in synthetic biology. We also introduced the relevant background of the International Genetic Engineering Machine Competition (iGEM) and the project theme of the participating team SJTU-Software to groups of students of different ages.

Course

We explained from the shallower to the deeper, started from the structure of DNA and RNA and combined it with the current social status of cancer prevention and treatment. We also introduced the content of our project---the DNA computing platform, and then transferred to the introduction of synthetic biology and the representative competition iGEM in this field. Students in class put forward their personal opinions on the term DNA computing, and understood the principle of DNA strand replacement reaction in our explanation.Through the process of teaching,we deeply felt the availability and demand of using vivid explanations to make students better understand the relevant knowledge in the field of biology.

We give lectures about iGEM and our programs to students in poor area in China

Through the course of career guidance, we introduced students the International Genetically Engineered Machines competition and the concept of synthetic biology from the profession of the scientist and told them about our project--the DNA computing platform.

After the introduction, most students became very interested in synthetic biology and were full of expectations,yearning for the field of biology. It’s estimated that nearly 70% of the students in the class said that they would like to become workers in the biological field in the future.

Hands-on

We asked students to use their wisdom to build the model of DNA and made them understand some basic knowledge of DNA such as the skeleton,bases and the Hydrogen bond force. They fully perceived the mode of double helix and experienced the charm of teamwork by the actual assembly.

Meanwhile,we let students make full use of their imagination to find out the probable structure of DNA and the method we could probably use to do the compute by means of shaping (pinching plasticine).

After that, we asked them to explain their work and analyze it. We hope that with the help of this course, students having interested in it could lay a simple foundation of synthetic biology and have an opportunity to get knowledge of the biology field.

Furthermore,we added some biological experiments in the hands-on part.Through making leaf vein bookmarks,students could explore the mysteries of biology from a macro perspective, find points of interest and further get knowledge of the combination and application of synthesis and biology by analogizing between the procedure and the one of gene knockout.

Brainstorm

In the free discussion session, students continued to have extensive discussions on the replication principles of DNA and RNA and the uses of DNA, and made their own ideas on how to better fight cancer.

During the brainstorm, students expressed their opinions imaginatively. We chose some representatives to give a speech and encouraged them to draw their own vision of the future of synthetic biology.Students actively participated in it.

Our activities have received unanimous praise from students and extensive attention from teachers and parents. Students all expressed the hope that there could be more similar courses and activities in the future to help them better understand the world of synthetic biology.

During the brainstorm, students expressed their opinions imaginatively.

Build an information classroom

We built some information-based classrooms for the local teachers and students to take advantage of and instructed them how to use the biological software,do virtual biological experiments and learn about the frontier knowledge and application of biotechnology.

Considering the close relationship between the development of bioinformatics and synthetic biology, and the convenience of information-based education,we put our emphasis on showing students how to use our virtual experiment software. We asked them to do it by themselves with the help of step-by-step learning guide.In that way,students could understand the experimental specifications and operating procedures.Besides,the way of software simulation let them ‘watch’ the contents of central dogma of molecular biology,gene pathway and so on individually.

Popular science website

In order to promote synthetic biology and to further promote the knowledge of early cancer screening, we have built a website for popular science introduction. After entering the website, you can intuitively understand the content of synthetic biology and early cancer screening, and you can also see the project team information. In the future, we will continue to improve the website and let it play a greater role.
